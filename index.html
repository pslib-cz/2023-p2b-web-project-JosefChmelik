About Us

At TenTech, we're more than just a computer store; we're a bridge between tradition and cutting-edge technology. We're a family-owned business with a rich history dating back to the early days of personal computing. It all started with our founder, Margaret Tenney, who believed in the power of technology to empower individuals and revolutionize the way we live and work. Margaret's dedication to building dependable, high-quality machines laid the foundation for TenTech. Today, her passion continues to be the driving force behind everything we do. We take pride in:

  • Hand-Crafted Excellence: Our pre-built computers are far from off-the-shelf. Each system is meticulously assembled and rigorously tested by our experienced technicians, ensuring exceptional performance and unwavering stability.
  • Timeless Durability: We source reliable components from trusted brands, prioritizing long-lasting performance over fleeting trends. We want your TenTech computer to be a trusted companion for years to come.
  • Personalized Touch: We understand that technology can be overwhelming. That's why our friendly and knowledgeable staff is here to answer your questions, recommend the ideal system for your needs, and offer ongoing support.

TenTech isn't just a store; it's a community. We foster a welcoming environment where tech enthusiasts and newcomers alike can feel comfortable exploring the ever-evolving world of computers.
